Medical Emergency

.........................................................................................................................................................................................................................................................................

When it comes to introducing medical emergency safety to children, you must make sure they understand the difference between a real emergency and a minor injury and/or situation. If they can sense a turn in someone’s health they should learn to get a response out of them and stay nearby. Introducing an emergency preparedness kit, also known as a first aid kit, and practicing the routine of a 9-1-1 call just in case of there being no responsible and known adult around is vital.

To make sure kids fully understand what to do during an unexpected medical emergency first explain what 9-1-1 is. Teach them to assess the risks before dialing 9-1-1 and explain what type of information to give once they have called 9-1-1. Another great tip is to practice scenarios with them making sure they’re more familiar with the concept without scaring them.
