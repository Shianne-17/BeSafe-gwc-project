Stranger Danger

Talking to your child of the importance of staying safe from stranger danger.

In today's world, kids need to know how to stay safe from strangers. You can educate your children about "Stranger Danger" by using a couple of these tips










One thing kids can use is the Buddy System. This makes sure that your child always has someone else with them so that they are not alone. Also make sure that your child reviews contact telephone numbers and home safety practices.











Teach them to trust your instincts as kids and to ask for help immediately if they feel like they are being followed or something is not right. Make sure that your child knows to do everything they can to attract attention, while yelling "Stranger Danger!" if a stranger grabs them.
